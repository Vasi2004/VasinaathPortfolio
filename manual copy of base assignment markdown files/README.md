# Assignment 1

There are two linked assignments. Assignment 1 and Assignment 2. The aim of these assignments is to get you to investigate a problem area while clearly and succinctly describing the problems and also proposing a solution. A lot of the assignment time will involve investigation, discussion and some research rather than coding / writing - in fact, there will be little to no coding.

Your Groups (Group 1, 2, 3, 4) in your workshops are there to determine what your Group A / Group B pairs are for this assignment. Please do NOT talk across groups. Just talk within your own team and with your paired team.

Team A will work on developing a SRS for Project A. 

Team B will work on developing a SRS for Project B. 

Team A will be the "client" / "stakeholder representative" for Project B 
- Team B will need to keep in regular contact to ask for extra requirements for Project B

Team B will be the "client" / "stakeholder representative" for Project A
- Team A will need to keep in regular contact to ask for extra requirements for Project A

## SRS Team

Your team has been made responsible for developing the Requirements for your major software project (Team A => Project A, Team B => Project B). You can access a group of stakeholders and any publicly available information that might help you. In Assignment 1, you will go through a round of requirements development over a 3 week period. Your team will gather whatever information you can, and develop a detailed requirements document for handover to the design team.

## Stakeholder Representatives 
Your stakeholders are currently working on another project, but are available to talk with you about your project. Simmilarly, your group is working on one project, but your group will ALSO be the stakeholders for their project. Plan your communication time and questions carefully as you only have a limitied time with them!

## Timeline: 
You will have some time in the workshops each week to work on the assessments, but you will also be expected to manage your time outside of the workshop times to complete the project.

  - __Week 4 Workshop group activity (part of the full workshop)__: Team inception, Team GitHub setup, and initial drafting of domain, interaction, use case diagram, use case description, requirements, and assumptions. (+ familiarise yourself with the other project and come up with some suggestions / "nice to have" features).

  - __Week 5 Workshop group activity (part of the full workshop)__: Teams interview each other and seek more clarification on vague parts of the spec / assumptions that have been made, also presents initial work for feedback / clarification. Discusses internally if changes proposed will be accepted.

  - __Week 6 Workshop group activity (part of the full workshop)__: 
Teams interview each other and seek more clarification on vague parts of the spec / assumptions that have been made, also presents initial work for feedback / clarification. Discusses internally if changes proposed will be accepted.

  - __Week 7 Workshop group activity (part of the full workshop)__: SRS and Video Handover between groups. (Getting Ready for Assignment 2)

  - __Week 8 Workshop *individual* activity (part of the full workshop)__: Assignment 1 Viva... on the SRS document your team received from your paired team.


While we gave you "some freedom" to select your own team, you can imagine that this type of assignment, with many student teams, requires careful planning. The Workshop Staff will do their best to facilitate the team formation process, but if circumstances change (e.g. group size issues, lack of cooperation, etc...), we may reorganise teams, and add or remove students. 

We will refer for the first assignment to the team working together for the first assignment as *SRS team*, and to the team of stakeholders as *Paired Stakeholder team*.

## Deliverables

### Your role as a SRS team 
As a member of the team of the SRS team, we expect you to:
 * As a team write an SRS document in the SRS document. A PDF version has to be uploaded on iLearn.
 * As a team produce a 3-minute recorded presentation and upload it to the deliverables folder. A copy has to be uploaded on iLearn.
 * Complete the individual reflection on iLearn.

### Your role as a Paired Stakholder Team 
As a stakeholder for the other project, we expect you to:
 * Cooperate with the other team on answering questions relating the requirements for their project in a timely manner.
 * Complete as a team of stakeholders the peer review form at the handover.
 * Participate in a viva in Week 8. This is a hurdle. The topic will be the SRS of the other team.
